# French Grammar Curriculum (Micro-Learning Approach)

This curriculum is designed to cover the entire French grammar spectrum from A1 (Beginner) to C2 (Mastery). It has been structured into **micro-lessons**, keeping in mind the app's pedagogical principle of 5–10 minute lessons. Each episode introduces a single grammar concept or a closely related pair to prevent learner overload.

## Module A1: Beginner (Lessons 1–30)
1. Subject Pronouns (*Je, tu, il/elle, on, nous, vous, ils/elles*)
2. The verb *Être* (to be) in the present tense
3. The verb *Avoir* (to have) in the present tense
4. Definite Articles (*le, la, l', les*)
5. Indefinite Articles (*un, une, des*)
6. Noun Gender (Basic rules and common endings)
7. Noun Plurals (Regular additions of *-s*)
8. Regular *-er* verbs in the present tense (Singular subjects)
9. Regular *-er* verbs in the present tense (Plural subjects)
10. Basic Negation (*ne... pas*) with *être* and *avoir*
11. Basic Negation with regular verbs
12. Asking basic questions (Intonation & *est-ce que*)
13. Question words: *Où* (Where) and *Quand* (When)
14. Question words: *Comment* (How) and *Pourquoi* (Why)
15. Adjective Placement (Before vs. After the noun - BANGS rule overview)
16. Adjective Agreement (Gender)
17. Adjective Agreement (Number)
18. Possessive Adjectives (Part 1: *mon/ma/mes, ton/ta/tes, son/sa/ses*)
19. Possessive Adjectives (Part 2: *notre/nos, votre/vos, leur/leurs*)
20. Demonstrative Adjectives (*ce, cet, cette, ces*)
21. The verb *Aller* (to go)
22. Prepositions of place with cities and countries (*à, en, au, aux*)
23. Contracted Articles with *à* (*au, à la, à l', aux*)
24. Contracted Articles with *de* (*du, de la, de l', des*)
25. The verb *Faire* (to do/make)
26. Expressions with *Avoir* (*avoir faim, avoir soif*, etc.)
27. Telling Time (*Quelle heure est-il ?*)
28. Days of the week and dates
29. Near Future (*Futur proche: aller* + infinitive)
30. Recent Past (*Passé récent: venir de* + infinitive)
